CIFRE PhD thesis Date published: 26 June 2026

CIFRE PhD opportunity at EXAIL Photonique and FEMTO-ST, Besançon

Composants et systèmes photoniques pour le monitoring environnemental

Start date
Autumn 2026
Host environment
EXAIL Photonique, Besançon, in collaboration with CNRS FEMTO-ST, Université Marie et Louis Pasteur.
Scientific topic
Development of new generations of photonic components and systems for environmental monitoring, combining lithium niobate integrated photonics and nonlinear effects in optical fibers.
Keywords
Photonics, lithium niobate, optical fibers, Brillouin sensing, environmental monitoring, nonlinear optics, integrated photonics.
Project focus
Distributed Brillouin sensors for remote measurement of temperature and strain over long distances, with applications on land, under the sea, or in space.
Candidate profile
Master’s degree or equivalent in photonics, physics, or a related field. Experimental and technological profile appreciated, with basics in numerical modelling, signal processing, clean-room technologies, and good English.
Contract
3-year fixed-term contract within the framework of a CIFRE PhD thesis.
Location
Besançon, France.
