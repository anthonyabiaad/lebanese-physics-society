François Englert (1932–2026)

Belgian theoretical physicist and Nobel laureate François Englert, co-originator of the Brout–Englert–Higgs mechanism, has died at 93. His work revealed how fundamental particles acquire mass and remains central to the physics explored at the LHC and beyond.
